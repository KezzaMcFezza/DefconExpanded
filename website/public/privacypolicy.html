Welcome to DEFCON Expanded. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website DefconExpanded or use our services.

Information We Collect

We collect only the following information when you register for an account on our Site:

- Email Address
- Defcon Username

We do not collect names, dates of birth, or any other personal information beyond email addresses and ingame usernames. We use your email address solely for the purpose of managing your account and communicating with you about our services.
And we only collect ingame usernames for leaderboard entries and statistic tracking for the username in question

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

- All passwords are hashed using bcrypt, ensuring we do not have access to your actual password.
- We regularly review and update our security practices to enhance data protection.

Data Retention

We retain your email address for as long as you maintain an active account on our Site. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including the right to access, correct, or delete your data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
