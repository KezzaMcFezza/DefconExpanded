16 Player Territories

After a long wait the 16 Player build is now ready for everyone to play and test! dont be hesitant to send in any bugs or issues that you find in the build as its just been released. If you want to know more about the 16 Player build in detail FasterThanRaito has made a dev blog which you can find here

The original DEFCON was split into 6 continent-like territories. With DEFCON Expanded, we are able to introduce more players and thus the need to divide the world into more parts. 8 Players attempt to keep as true to the vanilla experience as possible, but with 16 player it is simply not possible to adhere to purely geographic boundaries any more.

So, we have decided to divide the world along not just geographic lines but also regional cultural ones to make sensible factions which could plausibly present a united front while being balanced in territorial size relative to each other. Another factor is the number of cities in each territory. In default mode, each territory should have a minimum of 25 cities, but some parts of the world are so sparsely populated that no amount of pure territorial coverage would give them the required city count...
