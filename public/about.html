ABoUt thE Mod

Welcome to the Official Website for Defcon Expanded the 8-16 player mod for DEFCON. The mod is months in the making and wouldn't of been possible without some talented people who are passionate about the game. The mod is still in its infancy and I do suggest you email me, or contact one of us on the discord if any bugs or issues arise.

NEW GAMEPLAY FEATURES

Bomber Launch (Empty)

Previously, if you had bombers ready at an airbase or carrier and the base had available nukes in store, every launched bomber would be forced to arm itself with a nuke until the stores ran out.

The unintended consequence of this behavior is that rear airbases would end up with leftover nukes that couldn't be picked up for use until after the frontline bases had exhausted all of their supplies.

The new empty bomber launch mode allows you to launch a bomber without arming a nuke, allowing you to use the bomber to pick up a warhead at the base of your choosing.

There is no launch preparation cooldown between empty and armed bomber launch modes; if you are ready to launch one, you are ready to launch the other.

Mind that Airbases and Carriers do not have infinite storage capacity. Carriers cannot hold more than 6 free nukes (they start with 4 + 2 already armed bombers), and Airbases cannot hold more than 10 free nukes (they start with 5 + the 5 in armed bombers). This means if you already have maximum nuke storage at a base, you must launch an armed bomber and can no longer launch an empty one.

Color Picker

On opening the Alliance toolbar tab, you will see a new window appear that lists all of the team colors. The one highlighted in white is your current team color. The ones highlighted in black are the colors already in use by another team. Clicking on an available color will change your team to that color! Warning: this means you will defect from your current alliance if you have other team members who shared your original color.

Team Color Migration (Not Yet Implemented)

Eventually, we plan to use the team joining voting mechanism to allow multiple players on the same team to change to a new color simultaneously if they agree on it, instead of everyone having to individually defect from the original color alliance to the new color alliance.

NEW GRAPHICAL FEATURES

16 Available Team Colors!

Default colors were: Green, Red, Blue, Yellow, Cyan, Orange

New Colors are: Green, Red, Blue, Yellow, Cyan, Pink, Black, Orange, Olive, Scarlet, Indigo, Gold, Dark Teal, Purple, White, Brown

Also, the original colors have been adjusted slightly to better contrast with the game background and the other new colors.

Unit Outlines

To help make units more visible against the dark background of the world map, especially for darker colors and blue colors over the ocean, Unit Outlines creates a thin white highlight around every unit, improving visibility.

Radar Ghosting changes

Previously, enemy units that dropped out of radar view would turn grey. In order to prevent confusion with our new Black and White team colors, radar ghosts keep their original team color but now with a hashed pattern as they slowly fade into invisibility.

This also generally creates better contrast between actively visible units/buildings and those that have been previously scouted but are no longer under active view.

Chatbox Text Visibility enhancer

Chatbox Text has had its color intensity increased slightly to improve visibility over the dark game background, and certain darker colors also have an additional highlight box around the text to provide extra background contrast.
